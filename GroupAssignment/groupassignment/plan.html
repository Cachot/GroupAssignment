Mission Statement

We want to creat a website to promote the products of Frias, attract kids or their parents to view our site and purchase on the website, advertise the fashion events and new Café as well. We want to make pages more lovely to attract more young kids and their parents. We will also provide clear information for each products to show more details about our products.

Success Evaluation

1. The total views for the sites meet the requirement given by Frias.

2. The total profit from increased sale is bigger than the costs of the website.

3. The number of sign-up members meets the requirement given by Frias.
